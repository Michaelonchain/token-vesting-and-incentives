# Example Token Scenario

## Scenario
A DeFi protocol launching with a fixed max supply and controlled emissions.

## Parameters
- Max Supply: 10,000,000 tokens
- Initial Circulating Supply: 15%
- Annual Emission Rate: 8%

## Expected Outcome
- Gradual supply expansion
- Reduced sell pressure
- Strong long-term alignment

## Insight
Token design is more effective when incentives and vesting work together.
