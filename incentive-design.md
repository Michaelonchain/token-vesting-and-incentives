# Incentive Design Framework

## Principle
Incentives should reward behavior that strengthens the protocol.

## Incentive Types
- Staking rewards
- Usage-based rewards
- Governance participation incentives

## Design Goals
- Encourage long-term holding
- Reduce mercenary behavior
- Align users with protocol health

## Common Mistakes
- Over-incentivizing short-term actions
- Ignoring token velocity
