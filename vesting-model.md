# Token Vesting Model

## Objective
Ensure long-term alignment between contributors and the protocol.

## Sample Vesting Structure
- Team Allocation: 20%
- Cliff: 12 months
- Vesting Duration: 36 months (linear)

## Rationale
- Prevents early dumping
- Encourages sustained contribution
- Builds investor confidence

## Risk Considerations
- Overly short cliffs increase volatility
- Poor transparency reduces trust
